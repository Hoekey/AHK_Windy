monWorkArea [get]

Get the working area of a monitor in Pixel as a rectangle.

Same as monSize [get], except the area is reduced to exclude the area occupied by the taskbar and other registered desktop toolbars.  The working area is given as a rectangle.

Parameters

monMonitor number (Optional, Default: 1)

Remarks

  • There is no setter available, since this is a constant system property

See also
